Saving Lives

he National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) in India plays a crucial role in saving lives during disasters through various specialized operations. NDRF teams conduct search operations to locate survivors trapped under debris and carry out rescue operations using specialized equipment. NDRF assists in evacuating people from affected areas to safer locations, minimizing the risk of further harm.

Recovery Planning

Disaster recovery planning is primarily managed by the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) at the central level, in coordination with State Disaster Management Authorities (SDMAs) and District Disaster Management Authorities (DDMAs).
